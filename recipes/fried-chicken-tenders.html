Fried Chicken Tenders

Description

These are juicy, fried tendies that taste fantastic with almost any sauce you can think of (although I recommend buffalo sauce). Serve them with your favorite potato salad and corn on the cob.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 24 chicken tenderloins

Steps

  1. Place flour in a shallow bowl. Place breadcrumbs in a separate shallow bowl, and mix with ground black pepper and cayenne pepper. Place eggs in a small bowl.
  2. One piece at a time, coat the chicken in the flour, the eggs and the bread crumbs, and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. In small batches, fry chicken 6 to 8 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove chicken, and drain on paper towels.
